Make-A-Cup-Cake

Step One / 8

Shopping Time!

  • 150g white flour
  • 75g dark chocolate
  • 100g butter
  • 125ml boiling water
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp mixed spice
  • 3 Tbsp sour cream
  • 1 tsp instant coffee
  • Pinch of salt
Back Next Step

Step 2wo / 8

Mixing Time!

Mix together the flour, baking powder,

baking soda, mixed spice and salt.

Back Next Step

Step 3ree / 8

Melt, Add, Beat and Repeat!

Melt the butter and sugar.

Add one egg , together with a third of the flour mixture and a third of the sour cream

Beat untill these ingredients are well blended

Repeat untill the egss, flour and sour Cream are used up. :)

Step 6ix / 8

Preheat, Bake and Test!

Preheat the oven to 200 °C and line a muffin tray with paper cases. Fill each case with some batter and bake for 15-20 Minutes!. Test by inserting a skewer into the middle of a cupcake untill it comes out clean.

Back Next Step

Step 7even / 8

Be Cool!

Remove Cupcakes from the oven and from the metal tray. Leave Cupcakes to cool in the bin for about 5 minutes before setting them on a wire rack to cool down completely.

Back Next Step

Step Ate / 8

Cup-Cake-orator!

Unleash your inner Artist, Frost 3/4 of each cupcake with pink frosting frost the remaining frost in a lighter color. Pipe the cupcake in a swirley pattern up to the top! Place a sugar flower on top for an Retro feel, or keep it modern. You are the Artist, Enjoy!:)
